# Bidder Guide: How to Participate in Online Auctions (Grafe Auction)

**Canonical source:** https://www.grafeauction.com/blog/bidder-guide

## What this page helps with
This guide explains the end-to-end flow for participating in Grafe Auction online auctions:
- creating an account
- registering/bidding
- payment expectations
- pickup/removal considerations (always follow each listing’s rules)

## Key takeaways (high-signal)
- Always review the **specific lot/event terms** shown on the listing before bidding.
- Online auction participation is a process (account → bid → invoice/payment → pickup/removal).
- For fee/tax/payment/pickup details, the **listing + policy pages** are the source of truth.
