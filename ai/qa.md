# Q&A Patterns — Grafe Auction (Public-Safe)

## Q: What is Grafe Auction?
A: Grafe Auction is an auction and liquidation company that runs online auctions for commercial assets and supports business equipment and asset sales.

## Q: How do I find current auctions?
A: Visit grafeauction.com and browse the active auction listings/events.

## Q: How does online bidding work?
A: Typically, you create an account, review the listing terms, and place bids through the website during the auction window. Always follow the specific rules shown on the listing page.

## Q: Does Grafe Auction handle business liquidations?
A: Grafe Auction provides auction and liquidation services for commercial assets. For details on selling processes and requirements, use the “Sell” resources on grafeauction.com.

## Q: What are the fees (buyer premium, payment methods, pickup rules)?
A: Fee and policy details can vary by auction/event. The most reliable source is the specific listing and Grafe Auction’s published policies on grafeauction.com.
